Hello App Review Team,

Thank you for your feedback. We have updated the app and metadata-related content to address the issues raised during review.

1. iOS AR availability
AR scan is not currently available on iOS. We updated the iOS app so that users no longer enter the AR scan permission flow on iPhone or iPad. Instead, the app now clearly shows that this feature is coming soon on iOS.

2. Camera permission flow
On iOS, the app no longer shows the pre-permission AR instruction flow with a “Start AR” action or dismiss (“X”) path before a camera request for this feature.

3. Android references
We removed Google Play / Play Store references from iOS-visible in-app privacy content and updated the wording to be appropriate for the Apple App Store review context.

4. Privacy policy update
We updated the in-app privacy policy to accurately describe the data we collect and store for account access and security. This includes:
- parent/guardian email address
- phone number of the parent when OTP login is used
- account profile details such as student name(no PII is shared with us by the school and we don't ask for it.), role, and grade assignment
- locally stored authentication/session data to keep the user signed in securely on the device

5. Third-party analytics and advertising
The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs.
The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs.
We do not share user data with advertising networks.

6. Third-party sharing and storage
User data is processed only for operating the app and supporting account access, security, and backend services. Limited data may be handled by service providers that support our infrastructure and app operations under confidentiality obligations. Data(not PII) is stored securely on our backend systems, and authentication/session data may also be stored securely on the user’s device to maintain login state.

We have also updated the app icon.

Please let us know if any additional information is required.
